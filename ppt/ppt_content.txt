=== Slide 11 (Title) ===
  [IMAGE: picture 130]
  TEXT: 我们的核心库存是哪些品牌?
  TEXT: 它们的平均租金收益表现如何?
  TEXT: SELECT
  TEXT: brand,
  TEXT: COUNT(*) AS car_count,
  TEXT: ROUND(AVG(daily_rent), 2) AS avg_rent,
  TEXT: MAX(daily_rent) AS max_rent,
  TEXT: MIN(daily_rent) AS min_rent
  TEXT: FROM Car
  TEXT: GROUP BY brand
  TEXT: HAVING COUNT(*) > 1;
  TEXT: 分析逻辑：基于品牌进行聚合
  TEXT: (GROUP BY)，统计每个品牌的车辆总数、平均租金、最高租金和最低租金, 利用 HAVING 子句过滤出拥有多辆车的核心品牌。

=== Slide 16 (Title) ===
  TEXT: 基于派生表的查询：
  TEXT: 谁是累计消费超过1000元的 VIP 用户?
  TEXT: 通过子查询计算出每个用户的总消费金额
  TEXT: 在外层查询中筛选出总消费超过 1000 元的 VIP 用户
  TEXT: SELECT u.username, user_stats.total_spend
  TEXT: FROM User u
  TEXT: JOIN (
  TEXT: SELECT user_id, SUM(total_amount) AS total_spend
  TEXT: FROM RentOrder
  TEXT: GROUP BY user_id
  TEXT: ) AS user_stats ON u.id = user_stats.user_id
  TEXT: WHERE user_stats.total_spend > 1000;
